Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a “spin view”?

A spin view is just like a pageview on your website. So, when calculating the plan best suited for you, think about how many views your website gets currently a month.

What happens if I go over my view limit?

We will send you a notification over email when you're close, and then you'll have the option of signing up to pay for additional bandwidth allow your spins to show up as static images (your thumbnails) until the following month. Your current view count is always visible on your account page.

What if I need a custom viewer or other modification?

Please contact us at biz@arqspin.com. We can handle most any customizations and would love to hear about your project.

 
Can I use my normal camera or DSLR?

Yep. Arqspin doesn't just work with your phone, we support importing from any camera.

Why do I need a turntable?

To capture a high quality spin, you need the object to rotate at a fixed speed. Motorized turntables offer the easiest, most consistent way to do this.

What If I want to cancel?

We'd hate to see you go and would love to know why. But you can always cancel your subscription at any time through your account page. We only want people to pay for what they use.
