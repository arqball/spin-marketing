Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a “spin view”?

A spin view is just like a pageview on your website. So, when calculating the plan best suited for you, think about how many views your website generates in one month and how many pages you might display your spins on.

What happens if I go over my view limit?

We'll send you a notification over email when you're close, and then you'll have the option of signing up to pay for additional bandwidth or have your spins appear as static images (your thumbnails) until the following month. Your current view count is always visible on your account page.

I need a custom viewer or other modification. Can you help?

Yes. Please contact us at biz@arqspin.com. We can handle most any customizations and would love to hear about your project. We are commonly asked about on-premise hosting, custom viewers, custom iOS/Android apps, and other unique requirements.

 
Can I use my normal camera or DSLR?

Yep. Arqspin doesn't just work with your phone, we enable creating and sharing spins from videos captured with any camera.

Why do I need a turntable?

To capture a high quality spin, you need the object to rotate at a fixed speed. Motorized turntables offer the easiest and most consistent way to do this.

What If I want to cancel?

We'd hate to see you go, and we'd love to know why. But, you can always cancel your subscription at any time through your account page.
